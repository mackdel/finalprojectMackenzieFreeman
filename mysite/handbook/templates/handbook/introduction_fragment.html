Welcome to the Handbook

The ESI Employee Handbook serves as a comprehensive guide, outlining the policies, standards, and expectations for all employees. Its purpose is to foster a supportive work environment by communicating the rights, responsibilities, and benefits of employment at ESI.

This handbook, however, is not a contract of employment, and ESI reserves the right to modify or discontinue any policy as deemed necessary, with or without notice.

We encourage employees to familiarize themselves with these guidelines and consult Human Resources with any questions about the handbook's content.
