Welcome to Educology Solutions!

We’re thrilled to have you join our dedicated and talented team. At ESI, we pride ourselves on providing exceptional services to our government customers, and we believe that your time with us will be both rewarding and challenging.

This handbook is designed to help you navigate your journey here at ESI. It contains important information about our employment policies and benefits. While we comply with all federal, state, and local employment laws, this handbook primarily reflects those regulations and may not detail every local law.

We encourage you to read this handbook thoroughly. Once you've reviewed it, please sign the acknowledgment at the end to confirm that you've read, understood, and agreed to its contents. This handbook supersedes any previous versions or policy statements, and ESI reserves the right to update, modify, or supplement it as needed.

Remember, this handbook is a guide, not a contract of employment. It’s designed to address common situations in the workplace, but it can’t cover everything. If you have any questions or need clarification on any part of this handbook, please don’t hesitate to reach out to Human Resources.

We’re incredibly proud of our employees and the vital role each of you plays in making ESI a respected and trusted corporate citizen. Our work supports critical government missions, and together, we strive to exceed the standards set by our peers and competitors. Your commitment to our corporate policies and standards is crucial for both your growth and our collective success.

We’re excited to see all that you will achieve here at ESI. Thank you for being a part of our team!

Our Mission:

Modernizing Public Sector Services

As a provider of technology services and solutions, ESI strives to modernize and transform public sector services to create a digital-first experience. We help our clients create unified environments for development, continuous delivery/continuous integration, and integrate, automate, secure, and manage complexity through consulting, training, and other technical support services.

About ESI

Since its inception, ESI has grown into a distinguished IT services provider by recruiting highly skilled personnel, subject matter experts, technical specialists, and trainers. The company emphasizes robust quality management and closely aligns all solutions with government client missions.

Over the past decade, ESI has received numerous customer accolades and industry awards, experienced significant growth, and secured several key certifications and appraisals recognizing its process excellence.

Our Core Values

Employees first, customer always

We consider our team our most valuable asset. By ensuring our employees are fulfilled and engaged, we foster a collaborative and innovative environment that leads to superior solutions and heightened customer satisfaction.

You can’t manage, what you don’t measure

We strive to ensure that our actions are both correct and effective. By leveraging analytics, we enhance service delivery and elevate the quality and outcomes of our projects, business practices, and policies.

Mission driven leadership

Our leaders are seasoned and dynamic, energizing our team by aligning efforts toward a shared purpose. We seek leaders who clearly articulate our mission, champion our vision for the future, and define the critical role each employee plays in our collective journey.

Everyone matters

Diversity and inclusion are vital for enhancing business performance, especially in times of crisis. Our creativity and innovation stem from our diverse perspectives, enabling us to anticipate and adapt to changing consumer needs and prepare effectively for the future.
